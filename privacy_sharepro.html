Information Collection and Use.

For a better experience, while using our Service, we require you to provide us with some permissions such as Location, All file access(MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE), Read/Write permission, query all packages. These permission are required to share files(images, video, pdf, music files etc) between android devices and manage files on device. Please rest assured that we will not collect the content you share.

The app does use third party services that may collect information used to identify you such as Google Analytics for Firebase. Link to privacy policy of third party service providers used by the app Google Play Services, AdMob, Google Analytics for Firebase.

All Files Access Permission

One of the key features that sets SharePro apart is its ability to efficiently access and select files for sharing using the All Files Access Permission. This permission allows our app to access files stored on your device's external storage, providing you with full control over your digital assets.

Other Permissions

Location Permission - used by wifi direct for discovering near by devices.
Read/Write Permission - used for writing files on public folders internal/external storage.
Query all packages - used to discover which app are installed on device so that they can be shared with other Wifi direct connected users.
